## Overview

Codev_Tim — software engineer Timur ni ifodalovchi public engineering operating system. Next.js 15 modular sayt: product registry, uch tilli MDX pipeline, terminal-style navigation va contact workflows. Holat: In Development, v0.9.4.

Repozitoriy: [WinstonSalemm/Codev_Tim_site](https://github.com/WinstonSalemm/Codev_Tim_site) (public). Bu yozuv Railway da deploy qilinadigan shu codebase ni tavsiflaydi.

## Problem Statement

Oddiy portfolio shablonlari product architecture va engineering decisions ni yashiradi. Codev_Tim dedicated platform surface kerak: har bir mahsulot uchun Engineering Record, registry first-class navigation, trilingual content.

## Business Context

Auditoriya — technical buyers, engineering leads, hamkorlar. Trafik documentation-heavy: registry, project records, about va contact. Sayt — Codev_Tim brendining canonical public yuzi.

| Client  | Next.js 15, React 19    | App Router UI, registry, MDX              |

## Trade-offs

**Engineering OS vs portfolio.** Registry va architecture docs muhimroq.

**MDX in repo vs CMS.** File-based content; CMS keyinroq.

**Meta product registry da.** `codev-tim` slug repository ni ochiq hujjatlaydi.

## Roadmap

**Maintained.** Registry aniqligi, trilingual records, theme/onboarding, contact.

**Planned.** Tasdiqlangan production `SITE_URL`, Knowledge Base cross-links.

**Deferred.** Authenticated operator modullar.
